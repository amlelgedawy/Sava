\textbf{Test Scenario: Fall Detection.} A test subject wearing the MPU-6050 sensor on the wrist performed a simulated fall onto a cushioned mat within the camera's field of view. The SkateFormer model detected the falling posture and sustained the FALL prediction for 10 consecutive frames (about 0.67 seconds), while the accelerometer registered a brief free-fall phase followed by an impact spike of about 3.8g within the 500ms detection window. With both conditions met, the system issued a FALL DETECTED alert 1.1 seconds after the fall began and logged it with a timestamp. In a control trial, the subject raised an arm quickly to drink; the vision model briefly predicted FALL, but since the accelerometer recorded no free-fall and impact sequence, no alert was issued, confirming that the sensor fusion correctly rejected the false positive.
